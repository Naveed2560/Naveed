Simple Actions You Can Take

There are many small things we can all do to help reduce deforestation and protect our forests in Aotearoa and around the world. Here are a few ideas:

  • Recycle and reuse: Reduce waste by recycling paper, cardboard, and wood products.
  • Buy sustainable products: Look for products with eco-labels like FSC (Forest Stewardship Council) that come from sustainable forests.
  • Reduce meat consumption: Forests are often cleared for animal farming. Eating less meat can reduce the demand.
  • Spread awareness: Share information with friends and family about deforestation and why it matters.
  • Support organisations: Donate or volunteer with groups working to stop deforestation.
