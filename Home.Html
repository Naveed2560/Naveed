Intro

This site is aimed at making people realize what deforestation is all about, why it has become a major menace and what we can do to prevent it. You will be introduced to what deforestation is, its impacts on the planet, and the way Mangungungu concept of Kaitiakitanga can teach us to take care of the environment. No matter your age or interest, this site provides simple suggestions to conserve our forests and contribute to a greener future.

Take action now for a Sustainable Future

Educate yourself on the culprits of deforestation, its cultural beliefs such as Kaitiakitanga, and the small efforts we can make daily to save the environment — recycling, using sustainable products, and creating awareness. More than 80% of terrestrial flora and fauna live in forests. These animals lose their habitat when trees are felled, and we end up losing clean air and climate. Preservation of forests is the security of the world’s future.

Quick Fact: Around the world, a forest larger than 20 football fields is destroyed every minute.
