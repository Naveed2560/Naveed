Intro

This site is nothing but aimed at making people to realize what deforestation is all about, why it has become a major menace and what we can do to prevent it. You will be introduced to what deforestation is, its impacts on the planet and the way Mangungungu concept of Kaitiakitanga can teach us to take care of the environment. And no matter what your age or interest, this site provides simple suggestions and things you can do to conserve our forest and contribute to a greener future.

Take action now for a Sustainable Future

Educate yourself on the culprits of deforestation, its cultural beliefs such as the Kaitiakitanga, and the little efforts we can make each and every day to save the environment; this goes through recycling, using products that are sustainable, and creating awareness.More than 80 percent of terrestrial flora and fauna live in forests. These animals lose their habitat when trees are felled and we end up losing clean air and climate. Preservation of forests is the security of the future of the world.

Quick Fact: Around the world a forest larger than 20 football fields is destroyed every minute.
